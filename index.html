About me

Therefore, I studied application development during my three years of license. I chose this course because I love the world of code and challenges...Each year, I apply my skills acquired through internships, participating in hackathons for example "Global Game Jam 2018" and developing projects. Since I studied multimedia too, I discovered the importance of design. Now I know how to create ergonomic applications that give a better user experience and captivating graphics for the interfaces. I worke hard to developed the professional pillar by participating in various extra-university activities. What characterizes me is creativity, activeness, and rigorously. I am always looking to improve my personality and my technical as well as professional skills.
